Hi there
I'm a writer living on the coast.

I've also worked at nonprofits, restaurants, and bars from McDonald's to Tartine, rebuilt homes in New Orleans, and led a volunteer training for Lemony Snicket. I'm dedicated to equitable representation by and with marginalized people through narration, outreach, and development.

Research areas include political economy of coffee farmers, supply chains and alternative economic models, and postcoloniality.
